Tooltip Behaviors

Tooltip can be triggered on hover, focus, or other behaviors. Choose the right trigger based on your use case and accessibility requirements.

Hover Trigger (Default)

Best for: General information, non-critical context, desktop interactions

Click Trigger

Best for: Mobile devices, important information, persistent tooltips

Focus Trigger

Best for: Form elements, keyboard navigation, accessibility compliance

Behavior Comparison

Trigger Activation Accessibility Mobile Friendly Use Case
Hover Mouse enter/leave ⚠️ Limited ❌ No Desktop context
Click Mouse click ✅ Good ✅ Yes Persistent info
Focus Keyboard/mouse focus ✅ Excellent ✅ Yes Form elements
